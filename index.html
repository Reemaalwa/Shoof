How does it work?

With a simple activation, users unlock a powerful virtual assistant that helps them with everything from selecting color-coordinated outfits to identifying objects, reading labels, and even navigating unfamiliar environments.

Our Initiative

At Shoof, we are committed to changing this reality through cutting-edge AI-powered assistance. Our initiative harnesses the power of computer vision and real-time AI guidance to provide instant support, helping blind and visually impaired individuals navigate their world with confidence.

Why It Matters?

Independence should be a right, not a privilege. Shoof is more than just technology—it’s a movement towards accessibility, autonomy, and inclusion.

A Future Without Limits

Globally, over 285 million people are visually impaired, with around 39 million classified as blind. For many, simple daily tasks—like selecting an outfit, reading food labels, or navigating a crowded street—can present significant challenges.

Meet Founder

Driven by a passion for accessibility, innovation, and human rights, Reema Alwadi is the visionary behind Shoof. With a background in software development, ethical technology, and community education, Reema combines cutting-edge AI solutions with a deep commitment to social impact.
